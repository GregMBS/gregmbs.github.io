# Case Study: Mulroy for District Attorney, 2022

## The Challenge
Shelby County's District Attorney's office had been held by 
Republicans for years. Steve Mulroy was challenging an incumbent 
in a midterm cycle that was broadly unfavorable for Democrats 
nationally.

## The Role
GMBS Consulting served as Data Director for the Mulroy campaign, 
responsible for:
- Voter file analysis and targeting
- Precinct-level canvassing map development
- Volunteer territory assignment and field data operations
- Voter turnout modeling by demographic and geography

## The Result
Steve Mulroy won, flipping the Shelby County DA's office from 
Republican to Democrat in November 2022.

## What This Demonstrates
Winning a competitive race against an incumbent requires knowing 
exactly where your votes are, where your volunteers should be, 
and how to allocate finite resources across a large county. That 
is a data and mapping problem. We solved it.
